Minesweeper

Minesweeper is a logic-based puzzle game where players uncover tiles to find hidden mines. The numbers on revealed tiles indicate how many mines are adjacent. Clear the board without detonating a mine to win!

Breakout

Breakout is a brick-breaking game where you control a paddle to bounce a ball and clear bricks. Stay tuned for its release!

Snake

Snake is a classic game where you control a growing snake, trying to eat food while avoiding collisions with itself. More updates coming soon!
